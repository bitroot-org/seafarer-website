How are we helping?

Digital Files

Maintaining multiple documents have always been a hassle, we help a Seafarer digitise all type of files.

Seatime Management

Calculating Seatime in your head after every contract has been a habit to all Seafarers, we simplify that.

Learn and Grow

Upskilling is a process for all Seafarers in their journey. We help Seafarers with the necessary information to grow.

Secure Files & Documents

Carrying multiple physical documents on every path of a Seafarer's journey is tedious and full of hassle. Important files like, National CDC, COC, Visas, Contract Copy, Passport, GMDSS Certificate and many more need to be maintained. We give a digital space to maintain the same and remind the user incase of expiring documents.

NRI & Taxable?

Every Seafarer goes through a quality time investment to think every year or even months doing the same calculation, whether their residential status is of an NRI or if their income is taxable and if yes we help to find out that tax amount. These answers are now just a click away :)

Upskill & Updated

It's a frequent process for a Seafarer to keep growing and understanding which courses, which institutes and places are the next steps for them to upskill. We help you find DG approved institutes and keep in check with latest maritime updates.

Our overall offerings for the community

There are many aspects in a Seafarers journey that help them sustain a balanced career, we have tried catering to the problems that are faced upfront and are constantly growing for our Seafarers community.

FAQs

I’m not able to edit my details in the app!

You can contact us by writing on our WhatsApp Number +91-9892565103 or you can even contact us by filling the contact us option on the app or website (https://www.geofarer.com) or Help & Support option present in the app.

In “Residential Status Calculator” which dates should I enter?

Input your Passport dates if sailing on worldwide routes, Input your CDC dates if sailing on coastal vessels.
